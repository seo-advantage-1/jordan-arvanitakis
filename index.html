About

Jordan Arvanitakis is a digital artist specialising in creating stylised, illustration-based artwork that emphasises colour, shape, and personality over realism. His work focuses on capturing character and expression, resulting in visually striking pieces that feel modern and distinctive.

He produces high-resolution digital artworks designed for prints, wall art, and interior styling, making it easy to bring bold, animal-inspired visuals into any space without the need for custom commissions.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What do I receive when I purchase artwork?

You’ll receive a high-resolution digital file that can be downloaded instantly and used for printing or digital display.

What sizes can I print the artwork in?

Files are provided in high resolution, making them suitable for a range of print sizes from small frames to large wall pieces.

Can I use the artwork commercially?

Artwork is for personal use unless otherwise specified. Contact us for licensing options.

How do I print the artwork?

You can print at home, through a local print shop, or use online printing services for professional results.

Do I need a physical frame?

That’s up to you; artwork can be framed, mounted, or displayed digitally.
