About 

Jordan Arvanitakis is a digital artist who creates expressive animal illustrations. For Jordan, making art is a way to help people feel closer to nature. His work takes inspiration from the world around him - nature, animals, and the strong relationship they have with human beings.

From a powerful tiger to a loving family dog, Jordan creates beautiful pictures that add warmth to your home.

Jordan uses computer screens and digital brushes, carefully painting every detail on a tablet to generate realistic fur, feathers, and features. Using digital tools enables him to use colour and light in a way that makes each portrait look warm, happy, and full of life.
